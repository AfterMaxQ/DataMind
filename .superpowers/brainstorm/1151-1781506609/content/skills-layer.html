Example Skill: data-cleaning


  skills/data-cleaning/SKILL.md:

    # Data Cleaning Skill

    ## Purpose
    Systematically clean a raw dataset: detect issues, propose strategy,
    apply fixes, validate results.

    ## Inputs
    - dataset: name of raw dataset (from describe/)
    - constraints: optional user constraints (columns to preserve, etc.)

    ## Workflow

    Step 1: AUTO — Analyze
    AI reads describe/{dataset}.md + samples data
    → Produces: issue_report (nulls, outliers, type mismatches, duplicates)

    Step 2: GATE — Human approves cleaning strategy    ← HUMAN HERE
    AI proposes: "Drop null-revenue rows (23%). Forward fill regions.
                 Clip revenue to [0, p99]."
    Human: APPROVE | REJECT | MODIFY

    Step 3: AUTO — Execute
    AI generates scripts/clean_{dataset}.py and runs it
    → Saves: processed/{dataset}_clean.csv
    → Updates: graph.db (raw → clean edge), params.json
    → Appends: decisions.jsonl (why forward fill, why clip at p99)

    Step 4: AUTO — Validate
    AI runs validation checks on output
    → Produces: validation_report (null count, distribution shifts)

    Step 5: GATE — Human approves result               ← HUMAN HERE
    AI shows: before/after stats, validation report
    Human: APPROVE (proceed) | REJECT (re-run with changes)

    ## Outputs
    - processed/{dataset}_clean.csv
    - scripts/clean_{dataset}.py
    - validation report
    - Updated: graph.db, decisions.jsonl, params.json

The Human Role Shift

Without Skills
Human: "Clean the sales data"
AI: "How should I handle nulls?"
Human: "Drop revenue nulls, fill regions"
AI: writes code, makes mistakes
Human: "No, use forward fill not mean"
AI: fixes code
Human: "Also clip outliers"
AI: "Which columns? What threshold?"
Human: exhausted
With Skills
Human: /skill data-cleaning sales.csv
AI: executes Analyze → finds 23% nulls in revenue,
7 region typos, 1 negative revenue
[GATE] Proposes: drop null-revenue rows,
forward fill regions, clip revenue p99
Human: APPROVE
AI: executes, validates, writes everything
[GATE] Shows: before/after stats. Pass.
Human: APPROVE
Done. All context saved.

Complete Four-Layer Architecture

LAYER 4
Skills
Encoded workflows with human gates. Built-in: data-cleaning, data-exploration, feature-engineering, model-training, report-generation. Custom: fintech-sentiment, retail-forecast, etc. Skills compose into pipelines. Human only appears at approval gates — judgment, not labor.
LAYER 3
Assembly
Context Manifest — curated understanding for every AI session. Priority-ordered, token-budget-aware. Assembled from Layers 1+2. The bridge between stored knowledge and AI consumption.
LAYER 2
Cognition
WHY and what was learned. Decision log (what, why, alternatives). Exploration tree (dead ends + successes). Parameter registry (exact settings). Discovery feed (accidental insights).
LAYER 1
Lineage
WHAT happened to the data. Knowledge graph (Dataset, Script, Execution nodes). Script-as-edge pattern. Auto data.describe for every dataset.
